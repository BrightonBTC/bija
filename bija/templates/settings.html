Media uploads

Choose a service provider for media uploads:

The Nostr protocol itself does not allow for file uploads but media can be added to your posts by inserting a URL in to the text. A couple of Nostr specific cloud options have cropped up to support the community such as:

  • https://nostr.build
  • https://nostrimg.com

You can upload via their websites and then copy the generated links to your posts. Alternatively, add an upload button to your posts by choosing a different option from the dropdown above.

Add media uploads to your posts by adding a cloudinary account.

You'll need the name of your cloud and the name of an upload preset (create one in the Uploads section of your Cloudinary settings)
